Paths

You will need the sources for QDP++ and Chroma. I will assume that the QDP++ source is in /home/bj/src/qdp++ and that Chroma is in /home/bj/src/chroma. Naturally you will put them them in different directories on your machines. Just remember to substitute your various paths for /home/bj in the future.
